However, the shortage of growing far away from the family is always lacking an mentor, and that’s also why I love making friends, especially those people who has the values that I wish to develop, and luckily, I have made some great friends in my life so far.

I am an adventurer, I do not like to take people’s words for granted, which mean most of the time, I like to try it myself. The reason, I am doing that is not I do not respect those kind advice, is just the belief of everyone is different, I will and do keep their words in mind, so when I do fail, I will think about their advice again.And due to this belief, I have put myself into lots of interesting experience.

I love nature and appreciate the peacefulness it brings to me. I love lying on the grass and close my eyes enjoy the breeze and listen to the nature sounds. I love going to the beach and feel the ocean breeze or going there at sunrise. I love being around with friends who have positive attitudes towards life and always feel blessed being inspired by them.
