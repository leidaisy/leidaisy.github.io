Zen Village

2014

portfolio

This portfolio is the class project from Public Relations Writing and Design class with Dr. Maria Scott. The project requires us to work with a client and make PR publications for their needs. My client is the founder of the nonprofit organization- Zen Village- most people refer it as center. The center has huge financial problem due to it limits the target audience only to those underserved or elderly community in Miami Dade County. Therefore, my task with her is to rebrand the image of the center as bright and joyful and increase the community awareness of the center.
